About Duff Coin

Duff Coin is a cryptocurrency token that runs on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). The BSC blockchain uses a Proof of Stake consensus algorithm which means that $DUFF has a very low energy expenditure compared to proof of stake blockchains. POS also has many other benefits such as much faster and cheaper transactions.

The goal of Duff Coin is to replace the US dollar as the World Reserve Currency. It will allow for fast international transactions and eventually for interplanetary trade. There is a hard cap of 1 billion $DUFF tokens and inflation is 0%. It is extremely divisible, up to 18 decimals allowing for extreme scalability and any kind of trade.

The DUFF ecosystem is always expanding with the recent release of Duff Coin NFTs. New updates and developments coming soon, stay tuned for more details.
